Welcome to the new blog section of my portfolio. While the main portfolio showcases my AI prototypes, study guides, articles, and public appearances, this blog is where I'll share deeper reflections, tutorials, and behind-the-scenes looks at my work.

## What to Expect

Posts here will cover topics like:

- How I use AI tools like Claude and NotebookLM in my daily work as a Senior AI Business Analyst
- Lessons learned from enterprise architecture practice with TOGAF®
- Deep dives into the Business Analysis Knowledge Areas from the BABOK® Guide
- Behind-the-scenes on the AI prototypes featured in my portfolio

## Why a Blog?

The portfolio itself is excellent for showcasing *what* I've built. But the blog is for the *why* and *how* — the thinking process, the experiments that didn't make it into the portfolio, and the evolving perspective on AI in professional practice.

If you're a Business Analyst, Enterprise Architect, or just someone navigating the AI landscape in a corporate context, this is written for you.

Stay tuned.
